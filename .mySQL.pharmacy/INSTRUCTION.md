A pharmacy sells various types of products (medicines, cosmetics, etc.). In addition to the type, for each product there are
the code, designation, composition, manufacturer and sales price are recorded. When a customer buys a product for the first time
your data (name, address, telephone number, postal code, location and tax number) are recorded.
Once registered, whenever a customer purchases a specific product, their customer code is registered, the
products purchased, the name of the doctor (in the case of medical prescriptions), as well as the total sale. Each purchase is
identified by a number to facilitate database queries
